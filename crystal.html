Welcome to Crystal Collectors!!

• At the start of the game you will be given a random number, your goal.

• You will be presented with 4 crystals. Each crystal will be worth a specific number of points.

• Unfortunatley for you, those points are hidden until you select it.

• In order to win you must collect the exact numbers of points as your goal. If you go over, you lose.

• Your number and the values of the crystals will randomize each game.
